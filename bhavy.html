Introduction to Farm Equipments

Farm equipment helps farmers perform agricultural activities efficiently. Modern agriculture uses various machines to increase productivity and reduce manual labor.

Tractor

A tractor is the most important farm machine used for plowing, tilling and pulling agricultural machinery.

Plough

Plough is used for turning the soil and preparing the land for planting crops.

Seed Drill

Seed drill is used to plant seeds in the soil at the correct depth and spacing.

Harvester

A harvester is used to harvest crops like wheat, rice and corn efficiently.
